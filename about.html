🌍 About WasteWatchers

Snap · Scan · Save the Ozone

✨ What We Do

At WasteWatchers, we use Artificial Intelligence to help people identify different types of waste, guiding them towards responsible disposal and recycling. Our mission is simple — turn waste into wealth while saving the ozone layer.

👥 Who We Are

We are passionate tech enthusiasts and environmentalists who believe in harnessing AI for sustainability. WasteWatchers was built as part of World Ozone Day 2025, aiming to combine innovation with environmental responsibility.

🎯 Our Objective

Our goal is to create a platform that not only detects waste but also rewards individuals for disposing it correctly. By gamifying the recycling process, we inspire people to keep coming back, learn more, and contribute to a cleaner, greener future.
